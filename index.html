Different Types of Amplitude Exams

  • CAT

    The Common Admission Test is a computer based test held in India. The Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) started this exam and use the test for selecting students for their business administration programs.

  • GRE

    The Graduate Record Examinations (GRE) is a standardized test that is an admissions requirement for most Graduate Schools in foriegn countries.

  • Company Based Amplitude

    The difficulty level of exam vary from company to company.The test scores a candidate on the bases of Quantitative Ability (QA), Verbal Ability (VA) and Reading Comprehension (RC), Data Interpretation (DI) and Logical Reasoning (LR).
